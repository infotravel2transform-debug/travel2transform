Day 2 · Sun Oct 19

First morning yoga as the savannah wakes up. Then into Tarangire – Africa’s largest elephant herds and ancient baobab trees. This is where everything begins.

Day 3 · Mon Oct 20

Morning yoga at the crater rim. Then down into Ngorongoro – the world’s largest intact volcanic caldera. A natural paradise for the Big Five.

Day 6 · Thu Oct 23

Flamingos & Farewell

Final yoga. Lake Manyara – thousands of flamingos, tree-climbing lions. Farewell dinner. Departures October 24. You leave different. The wilderness stays with you.
